## Recommended Colors
- Window frame: #7f849c (gray)
- Progress bar (filled): #a6e3a1 (green)
- Progress bar (empty): #45475a (dark gray)
- Checkmarks: #a6e3a1 (green)
- Title: #cdd6f4 (light gray)
- Tagline: #9399b2 (medium gray)
- Background: #1e1e2e (dark)
